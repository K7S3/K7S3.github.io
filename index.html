About Me

I'm a passionate AI researcher and software engineer with a strong background in machine learning, computational biology, and full-stack development. Currently working at Prudential Financial, I focus on building innovative solutions that bridge the gap between cutting-edge research and real-world applications.

My journey in tech began at Cornell University, where I developed a deep understanding of computer science fundamentals. Since then, I've worked on diverse projects ranging from GPCR activation mechanisms to modern web applications.
