About Me

I'm a passionate AI researcher and software engineer with expertise in machine learning, computational biology, and full-stack development. Currently serving as a Senior Machine Learning Engineer at Prudential Financial, where I develop cutting-edge AI systems including real-time Table Augmented Generation (TAG) and automated GenAI validation frameworks.

My academic journey includes a Master's from Cornell Tech and a Bachelor's from IIIT Hyderabad in Computer Science and Computational Natural Sciences. I've contributed to open-source projects through Google Summer of Code, published research in the Journal of Chemical Education, and worked as a Mitacs Globalink Researcher at Queen's University.

As the Technical Founder of Synergii, I built an AI-powered grant discovery platform that addresses a $34 billion market opportunity, gaining 1000+ requests within two weeks of launch. My passion lies at the intersection of research and entrepreneurship, using AI to solve real-world problems.

AI-powered grant discovery and application platform for researchers & entrepreneurs

Synergii is an AI-based grant search and apply platform that helps researchers and entrepreneurs find the most relevant grants and apply to them in an instant. We help entrepreneurs, non-profits and SMBs with the tedious task of discovering the most relevant grants and streamlining the application process for them in just one click.

Built this innovative tool from the ground up as part of Cornell Tech's startup studio course. Designed in Figma and developed the MVP from 0 to 1, integrating public datasets to capture a $34 billion market opportunity with targeted early revenue projections of $85 million.
